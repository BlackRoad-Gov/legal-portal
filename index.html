Legal Infrastructure for Founders

BlackRoad OS, Inc. was formed as a Delaware C-Corp through Stripe Atlas. We built Legal Portal because we needed it ourselves. Every feature solves a real problem we faced.

📜

SEC Filing Tracker

Track all required filings with automated deadline reminders. Form 1120, quarterly estimates, state annual reports, beneficial ownership reports. Never miss a filing deadline again. Calendar integration with Google Calendar and iCal.

🔒

GDPR / CCPA Compliance

Data mapping tool to inventory all personal data your product collects. Automated privacy policy generator. Data subject request workflow. Cookie consent management. Breach notification templates with regulatory timelines.

📄

Contract Management

Template library for common startup contracts: employment agreements, NDAs, contractor agreements, SaaS terms, vendor agreements. Version control for all documents. Signature tracking with expiration alerts.

IP Portfolio

Formation Timeline

Based on BlackRoad OS, Inc.'s own formation. A real-world timeline of what it takes to go from idea to incorporated.

Day 1 Complete

Certificate of Incorporation

Filed with Delaware Division of Corporations. 10M shares Common Stock authorized at $0.00001 par value. Registered agent: Legalinc Corporate Services Inc.

Day 1-3 Complete

EIN & Board Actions

IRS Form SS-4 submitted. EIN received. Initial board actions: adoption of bylaws, appointment of officers, authorization of stock issuance, banking resolution.

Day 3-7 Complete

Stock & Equity

Restricted Stock Purchase Agreement executed. Stock certificates issued. 83(b) election filed with IRS within 30-day window. CIIAA signed by all founders.

Day 7-14 Complete

Banking & Stripe

Corporate bank account opened. Stripe Atlas integration for payment processing. Accounting system setup. Chart of accounts configured for Delaware C-Corp.

Day 14-30 Complete

IP Assignment & Indemnification

Intellectual property assignment agreements executed. Indemnification agreements for officers and directors. Form 8821 filed for tax representative authorization.

Ongoing Active

Checklist

Legal Portal generates a personalized compliance checklist based on your entity type, state of incorporation, number of employees, and business model. Track progress in real-time and get alerts before deadlines.

This isn't a static document. It's a living dashboard that updates as regulations change, as your company grows, and as new compliance requirements emerge. Built by founders who believe legal compliance shouldn't require a law degree.

  • Certificate of Incorporation filed
  • EIN obtained from IRS
  • Bylaws adopted by board
  • Stock authorized and issued
  • 83(b) election filed (30-day window)
  • CIIAA signed by all founders
  • Corporate bank account opened
  • Privacy policy published
  • Terms of service published
  • Beneficial ownership report filed
  • State annual report filed
  • Delaware franchise tax paid
  • Form 1120 filed by April 15
  • Worker classification reviewed
